Snickerdoodle

Description

A snickerdoodle is a cookie rolled in cinnamon sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 teaspons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, slightly softened
  • 1/2 cup whipped honey with cinnamon
  • 1 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350, prepare baking sheets with tinfoil.
  2. In large bowl, mix together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, salt.
  3. In another large bowl, mix butter, honey, 1 cup sugar until light and fluffy. Then, add eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix.
  5. Ins mall bowl, combine remaining 1/3 cup sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Create balls with the cookie dough (about 2 teaspoons) and roll throughougly through the smaller bowl mixture.
  7. Bake for 9-10 minutes.
  8. Use reamaining small bowl mixture to top cookies.
