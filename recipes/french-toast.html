French Toast

Description

French toast is a dish that consists of slice bread soaked eggs and milk. The bread is then pan-fried. Toppings typically consist of cinnamon and syrup.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • pinch of salt
  • bread
  • butter

Steps

  1. In mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Pour mixture into shallow glass baking dish.
  3. Put pan on stove voer medium heat, melt 1-2 teaspoons of butter.
  4. For each piece, dip it in mixture for 3 seconds per side.
  5. Place piece on pan and cook for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.
