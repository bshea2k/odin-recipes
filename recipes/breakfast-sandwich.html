Breakfast Sandwich

Description

A breakfast sandwich consists of eggs and bacon between bread. Cheese can be optionally added.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bacon

Steps

  1. Crack the 2 eggs into a small mixing bowl and mix.
  2. Toast the 2 slices of bread.
  3. Put bacon on frying pan and cook.
  4. Once the bacon is cooked, remove bacon and immediately put the eggs in the pan.
  5. Flip eggs once bottom is golden or cooked.
  6. After flipping, turn off heat to stove and let eggs sit for a minute.
  7. Form breakfast sandwich.
